educational background

Just a disclaimer: My school life is nothing to brag about. Please don't expect anything extravagant. Please treat this like a personal diary as well (lol). I didn't include my kindergarten life. Long story short, we drew apples, counted numbers, and wrote abc's, played outside until mud was like our body soap hehe. Let's move on...

Elementary

I started elementary a year earlier than the average kid. I went to Immanuel Mission International School from first grade up until fourth grade. My sister graduated so I decided to transfer schools as well. For fifth grade I took an exam for City Central School. I passed, but sadly my name wasn't anywhere to be seen in the list of students for the sections so I was enrolled in a regular section. It was only found out that I was accidentally enrolled to a higher grade (sixth grade), and it was in the middle of the school year too. I just decided to continue. For my sixth grade me and my parents decided that I should transfer, and so I found myself learning a lot in COC. Basically COC was the source of my knowledge for passing my High School choice exam.

Achievements

  • Graduated LOL
  • Received a lot of medals for 1st to 4th grade, they were generous!
  • Finished 1st Honorable Mention for 6th grade!

Junior High School

For Junior High School (at this time this term still did not exist yet), I took an exam for Gusa Regional Science High School-X. When the results were out, only my mom went out to check them. I was pretty anxious about the results because I wasn't able to review AT ALL. But my mom was more anxious between us two. I remember her arriving home and telling me that she was scanning through the results paper starting from top 20+ up and did not find my name. She repeated it until she decided to check from the first paper, and apparently I was 15th! It might be the only thing I'll ever be proud of in my whole life, I thought. So four years passed and I survived the hell that was GRSHS-X, but it was fun going through it with the same section (there were no shuffling). There were lots of activities as well, both academic and non-academic!

Achievements

  • Moved Up Hahahahaha
  • Got a good standing in the section
  • Nothing worth mentioning actually

Senior High School

For Senior High School, a lot of us were set on staying in GRSHS-X, me included. Somehow it was the best option for us that time. And of course I did not regret it. Hard the training might be, it produced us to be more than qualified for college at least(?). We were shuffled, at first we expected it not to end well but apparently the sections we had were all gucci! The new curriculum was a bit chaotic since we were the first ones to experience SHS. Our batch unanimously think we did not learn a lot because of this, sadly.

Achievements

  • Graduated AGAIN of course
  • Miraculously graduated as the 4th in our school lol
  • Did nothing significant, this is an achievement in itself

College

For college I took exams and passed them. I was having second thoughts of where to enroll. I was thinking of UPD but I thought of many lazy things and I was too scared to adjust to new things lol. My options were USTP and MSU-IIT then. I enrolled for MSU-IIT already. Computer Science. During vacation though I took a lot of time thinking of things that might actually happen during college. And so for the last minute (it was nearly June or so), I told my mom what if I withdrew my enrolment in IIT and go for USTP instead. She was delighted and immediately went with me to do the deed. At last I'm here in USTP. I hope I won't be regretting it. Sometimes I think of what-ifs...
